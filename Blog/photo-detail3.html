Mountains

I took this picture because the scene felt huge and peaceful at the same time. The mountains, the dark blue water, and the heavy sky looked so dramatic that I didn’t want to miss the moment. It felt like the weather was about to change, and that made the view even more interesting. The colours made me feel calm but also a bit amazed. The deep blues and greys looked powerful, and the green trees in the front added a softer, warmer touch. It’s the kind of view that makes you stop for a second and just breathe it in.
